Our solution to save the rainforest

Why it's important to save the rainforest

The way we humans are treating our earth could be fatal for our planet’s future. Due to lack of sustainable consumption and production, the climate is running into a downward spiral towards the point of no return. Ruining nature - the origin of our existence - is mankind’s greatest mistake, but if we act now, we can gain a step towards the right direction.

Our Solution

Introducing the Rainforest Lunchbox.

Our solution is a product that will make it possible to easily restore deforested rainforest by turning the inhabitable habitable.
The Rainforest Lunchbox does so by simulating the climate at the bottom of the rainforest.

Help us save the rainforest

We are going to seek funding from countries and their government that can benefit from the product. That includes countries such as Brazil and Singapore. Brazil’s government and World Wildlife Fund has pledged 215 million dollars to save 150 million acres of brazilian rainforest (1) .
