Our solution to save the rainforest.

Turns unhabitable grounds habitable

As the rainforest is cut down, what is left is a barren plain of dried mud and clay whithout any source of neutriants, as the rain washes the biomass away. The cracking crust is inhabitable for any plants that belong to the tropical forest. Whith the Rainforest Foodbox, this is no longer the case.

Decrease death rate

The Rainforest Lunchbox is designed to provide neutrients, keep the humidity inside the soil and the shade cloth will protect the plants from sunlight, as they are adapted to growing in the shade of the forest. This makes the dried crust humid, and the artificual biomass that is the Rainforest Lunchbox will make it possible for our plants to grow strong.
